Third-party logistics providers require different platform architectures than single-shipper operations, with five critical failure modes including aggregate optimization that ignores customer constraints and lack of service tier orchestration during capacity shortages. AI-driven systems address these multi-tenant complexities through customer-aware optimization and automated onboarding workflows. The article contrasts standard TMS solutions against purpose-built 3PL platforms that can handle the operational complexity of serving multiple customers simultaneously.
