AI-powered dispatch and routing systems can absorb fuel cost pressures through six operational levers including miles reduction, empty miles elimination, and density-aware sequencing rather than passing expenses to customers. These combined strategies typically produce 15-25% fuel cost reductions, allowing businesses to maintain pricing stability during commodity price volatility. By protecting customers from fuel surcharges, companies gain a competitive advantage as fuel price fluctuations become an increasingly important differentiator in logistics markets.
