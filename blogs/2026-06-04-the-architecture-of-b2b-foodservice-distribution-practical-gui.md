European B2B foodservice distribution presents unique operational challenges including multi-temperature deliveries, high SKU velocity, tight pre-opening windows, and daily delivery cadence that generic logistics platforms struggle to address. AI and agentic architecture are well-suited to handle five key operational layers: multi-temperature route optimization, substitution decisioning, predictive demand and capacity planning, driver-customer relationship intelligence, and perishable exception management. Operations leaders should evaluate logistics platforms on their ability to handle these complexities natively rather than treating foodservice requirements as edge cases.
